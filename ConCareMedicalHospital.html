Welcome to ConCare Medical Hospital

The ConCare Medical Hospital, in Banga, Aklan, is widely recognized as one of the leading medical centers in the world,
offering a vast array of advanced technology medical services.
We offer variety of amenities and services to make the patient experience as positive as possible that prioritizes patient comfort and well-being.
Whether you are seeking advanced treatment, for a complex condition or preventive care,
ConCare Medical Hospital offers a worl-class healthcare experience with cutting-edge technology and patient-centered approach.


Clinical Services Offered:

ConCare Hospital is equipped with the latest advancements and dedicated professionals to get you back on your feet in no time.
From minimally invasive surgery to robotic-assisted procedures, ConCare is at the forefront of healthcare innovation.
Experience precision, speed, and shorter recovery times with our advanced medical services.


Check out our full range of services and find the perfect fit for your needs:

- Clinical Services
- Laboratory Services
- Clinical Diagnostic Tests
- Public Health and Medical Assistance Services

Other health services offered:

- Educational Training, Research, and HRD Services
